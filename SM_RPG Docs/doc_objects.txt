MOLD     = object's mold

byte 1,2 (9-bit) - sprite/palette pointer

HOW TO ARRIVE AT OFFSET:

AND pointer by $01FF
    AND'ed value: multiply by 4 and add to 251800 for palette/sprite pointers
AND high byte of same pointer by $0E
determine operator from this list, where AND is value AND'ed by $0E and #$ is corresponding operator
AND  #$
-------
00   00
02   1E
04   3C
06   5A
08   78
0A   96
0C   B4
0E   D2
add #$ for AND'ed value to palette pointer at $251800 (2nd 16-bit word)

C2/7127 BF 00 00 E5 LDA $E50000,x[$E5:06C8] ;load make-up pointer
C2/712B 85 D8       STA $D8    [$00:00D8]   
C2/712D 29 FF 01    AND #$01FF              ;isolate bottom 9 bits
C2/7130 0A          ASL A                   ;multiply x4
C2/7131 0A          ASL A                   
C2/7132 AA          TAX                     
C2/7133 BF 00 18 E5 LDA $E51800,x[$E5:192C] ;load mold
C2/7137 85 DC       STA $DC    [$00:00DC]   
C2/7139 BF 02 18 E5 LDA $E51802,x[$E5:192E] ;load palette pointer
C2/713D A6 D0       LDX $D0    [$00:00D0]   
C2/713F 9F 13 00 40 STA $400013,x[$40:4013] ;store as palette pointer

C2/718B A5 D8       LDA $D8    [$00:00D8]   ;make-up pointer to offset bank pointer
C2/718D EB          XBA                     
C2/718E 29 0E 00    AND #$000E              ;isolate and make number even
C2/7191 AA          TAX                     
C2/7192 BF F0 73 C2 LDA $C273F0,x[$C2:73F4] ;load bank offset pointer
C2/7196 A6 D0       LDX $D0    [$00:00D0]   
C2/7198 E2 20       SEP #$20                
C2/719A 9F 15 00 40 STA $400015,x[$40:4015] ;store as bank offset pointer

C2/88B4 BF 15 00 40 LDA $400015,x[$40:4015] ;load bank offset pointer
C2/88B8 29 FF 00    AND #$00FF              ;isolate
C2/88BB 18          CLC                     
C2/88BC 7F 13 00 40 ADC $400013,x[$40:4013] ;add to palette pointer
C2/88C0 AA          TAX                     
C2/88C1 A9 1D 00    LDA #$001D              ;load palette size
C2/88C4 8B          PHB                     
C2/88C5 54          MVN 00 E5               ;copy palette to ram

GFX OFFSET:
AND #$000F + #$280000 = bank of GFX
AND #$FFF0 + bank = address of GFX
