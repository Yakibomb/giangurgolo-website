SPD    = Speed
         ;of all entities in battle, speed is arranged lowest to highest
         ;each attacker takes their turn in accordance to their speed
ATK    = Attack
DEF    = Defense
M.ATK  = Magic Attack
M.DEF  = Magic Defense
EVD   = Evade Rate
M.EVD = Magic Evade Rate
         ;only effective if a character's spell does NOT have a 100% hit rate
         ;only spells affected: Sleepy Time, Mute, Terrorize, Poison Gas
SPEC   = Special Flags
         HLD: if HP below 0, do not remove monster
         PAL: affects the palette? used only by the four Crystals
PRT    = Protection: success ratio of sheepify items and Yoshi Cookie
D      = protected against instant death (Y = yes, N = no)
